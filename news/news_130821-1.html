Calaeus Presents: Birth of Data Node Zero


Well preserved archive of DN1 website gave birth to an idea of a community information hub for mxoemu. As such, with the skeleton and skin of the original, this website has been created and named "Data Node Zero".

Data Node Zero will serve as an mxoemu unofficial community website with all kinds of emu related news, guides and other useful information.
